Xtream Chaser

Xtream Chaser is a fighting genre game with full animations and unique game battle modes. For all of you who are fans of the fighting game genre with a 3D view. We call all Heroes who definitely want to join, exploring and adventuring, also play together with other Heroes exploring the world of Xtream Chaser. Explore this world, find unique items and use the heroes you use. As an online game, of course Xtream Chaser presents interesting events along with interesting and very exciting types of gameplay.

Enter the world of Xtream Chaser and meet all the heroes in the world. Choose the hero you want and even you can play them all. Raise their level and become a true hero. Gather various types of gear and use it on your hero. Explore this world and become the best hero by fighting with other heroes, fighting bosses in dungeons, fighting between guilds to improve guild ranking and of course solo match ups to get the title of the strongest Hero in the world of Xtream Chaser. What are you waiting for ? Come and join us Heroes!
