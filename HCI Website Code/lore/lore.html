Lore

The Story of Xtream Chaser World

The story begins on earth where a genius man was born this world. His name was Professor Afro. Time passed by untill now he's a very genius man who conquered all geniuses from around the world.

Right now Prof Afro beginning his research and looking for some hidden information about any other worlds out there. Earth was a very peaceful world, and one time monsters and evil creatures appear out of no where. The evil Grim Reaper who controlled this world and he name this world as Xtream world. All humans will be turned into monsters and those monsters acts as the Grim's soldier who obey all of the Grim's order. Luckily Prof Afro managed to escape and hide in the secret base that he found and make it as his own Headquarter. The situation of this world, is very dangerous. Grim's isn't the only main evil boss, but there's also Snowman Boss who controlled the Snow Village, Forest Guardian who controlled the forest village, and Fire Spirit who controlled the Fire Kingdom. Professor Afro needs to act how can he save this world from disaster.

Time passed by and he founded a clue while searching secretly in this world. He found out that there are many Heroes out there from their own world who is very skillful and powerfull enough to defeat the Grim. Therefore, he make a Portal Gate to summon them. He's trying so hard with so many fails on the portal. One time he test the portal, and magically the portal was a success. He managed to summon heroes and the heroes are willing to help in order to save this world from the disaster. These heroes are the best from their world. The current summoned heroes are Cheng, Barbarian, Musket Lori, Piper Archer, Volcano, Frost, Ninja Lanaya, Claw Sylvanna and Trnity Heal. The are very ambitious to battle with the Bosses from this Xtream worlds. They also has goal in mind, the goal is to become the strongest Hero in the universe..
